Welcome to Kaye's Coffee

At Kaye's Coffee, we believe in the magic of a great cup of coffee. Every sip is crafted with passion, using the finest beans and ingredients to bring you the perfect balance of flavor and warmth. Whether you're here for a morning boost, an afternoon treat, or just a cozy space to relax, we welcome you with open arms. Enjoy the aroma, take a sip, and feel at home. ☕❤️

The Story of Kaye’s Coffee

Kaye’s Coffee is a dream come true for Cheskaye Fugaban, a coffee lover who wanted to create a cozy place where people can enjoy great coffee and relax. She named it Kaye’s Coffee, inspired by her own name, to make it feel personal and special.

Opening on April 10, 2025, Kaye’s Coffee is more than just a café—it’s a warm and welcoming space where everyone can feel at home while sipping their favorite coffee.

Our Vision & Mission

🎯 Mission
